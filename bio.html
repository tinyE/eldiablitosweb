EL DIABLITOS is a spaghetti-western-like power trio hailing from Portland Oregon.

Tony Guerrero, hailing from San Francisco, is the founder of the sound. Using a big-clean stereo guitar, he belts out twangy, soul piercing guitar voicings that are the trademark sound of the band.

From the high desert of Eastern Oregon on drums is Don 'Vito' Griswold who's Bruce Lee philosophy is to use "No Way as Yes Way".

Brett 'Bolillo' Edelen, originally from Texas, ties it all together with the steady, flowing low sounds of the bass

The band lives by a credo of 'Don't think, feel - don't think, do' which allows these three to create a gelling sense of swirling, twangy, reverbery, spooky, upbeat and noir-like tunes that win over crowds in just about every genre and age group.

While most bands tend to need vocals EL DIABLITOS use the melodies of the guitar, and the spirit of the music to penetrate minds, hearts and souls, in a heavy sounding Link Wray-type way that their music is sure to carry forward just as the man himself did when he first slung a guitar.
