EL DIABLITOS is a quasi-spaghetti-western-like quartet from Portland Oregon.

Tony 'ARG' Guerrero, originally hails from San Francisco and is the founder of the bands sound. Using a big-clean stereo guitar, he belts out twangy, soul piercing guitar voicing’s that are the trademark sound of the band.

From the high desert of Eastern Oregon keeping the backbeat on drums with a hint of electronica is Don 'Vito' Griswold.

Brett Edelen, originally from Texas, ties it all together with the steady, flowing low sounds of the bass.

And lastly adding Rose City native Pamela Flores’ soothing soulful voice on selected songs and you have a well-blended mix of swirling, twangy, reverbery, spooky, upbeat and noir-like tunes that win over crowds in just about every genre and age group.
